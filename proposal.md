**Brainstorm Questions**
### Problem Discovery

- Who has the problem?
- What exactly is painful?
- When does it happen?
- How often?
- How serious is it?
- Who pays the cost?
- Who benefits if we solve it?

### Current Workflow

- What are the current steps?
- Which step is manual?
- Which step takes the longest?
- Which step causes the most mistakes?
- What tools are currently used?

### Root Cause

- Why does this problem exist?
- What information is missing?
- What decision is difficult?
- What is repetitive?

### AI Fit

- Can software without AI solve this?
- If yes, why use AI?
- What reasoning does AI perform?
- What input does AI need?
- What output creates the most value?

### Solution

- What's the one-sentence idea?
- Why is it better than today's solution?
- What's the biggest "wow" moment?

### MVP

- What's the smallest thing proving our idea?
- What can we fake?
- What can we remove?
- What must work live?

### Demo

- What does the audience see first?
- What does the user click?
- What happens in under 10 seconds?
- What is the final output?
- What makes judges remember us?

### Business

- Who inside the company buys this?
- What KPI improves?
- Does it save money, make money, or reduce risk?
- How would this integrate with existing systems?
- Why would the company actually adopt it?
